About us

We are six Communication Design students from Politecnico di Milano, and this project was created as part of the Computer Graphics for Information Design course. Our team worked collaboratively to combine our skills in data processing, interactive design, and visualization, aiming to present a complex narrative in a user-friendly and visually appealing way.
